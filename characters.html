Characters of Westeros

Search and discover your favorite heroes, villains, and legends.

Jon Snow

Raised as Ned Stark's son, Jon Snow becomes a key leader of the Night's Watch and protector of the realm beyond the Wall.

The last known Targaryen princess, Daenerys rises from exile to become the Mother of Dragons and a powerful claimant to the Iron Throne.
